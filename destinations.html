Major Tourist Destinations

Kathmandu

Kathmandu is the capital city of Nepal and the cultural center of the country. It is famous for historic temples, palaces, and traditional architecture.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a scenic city known for its lakes and stunning views of the Annapurna mountain range. It is a popular base for trekking.

Lumbini

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists from around the world.

Chitwan

Chitwan National Park is famous for jungle safaris and wildlife conservation, including rhinos and Bengal tigers.

Mt. Everest

Mt. Everest, locally known as Sagarmatha, is the highest mountain in the world at 8,848 meters. It is a major attraction for trekkers and mountaineers, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas and a chance to experience Sherpa culture.
