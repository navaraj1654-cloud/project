Welcome to Nepal

Nepal is a country rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage. Located between India and China, Nepal offers a unique travel experience with its mountains, temples, and diverse traditions.

Why Visit Nepal?

Nepal is home to Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, and several UNESCO World Heritage Sites. It is a perfect destination for adventure, spirituality, and cultural exploration.
