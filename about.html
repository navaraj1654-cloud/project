About Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, bordered by India and China. It is known for its geographical diversity, ranging from the Himalayan mountain range to plains and hills.

Nepal has a rich cultural history influenced by Hinduism and Buddhism. The country celebrates many festivals throughout the year, reflecting unity in diversity.

Tourism is an important sector of Nepal’s economy, attracting visitors from all over the world for trekking, pilgrimage, and cultural exploration.
